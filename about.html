About the election system

The University Student election system is design to provide student with secure, transperent, and fair platform for electing their student leaders. The system promotes democratic participation and ensure every eligible student has the opportunityto vote.

Mission

To provide a reliable, transparent, and efficient studentelection process that encourages active participation and responsible leadership.

Vision

To become a trusted digital election plaatform that strengthens student democracy and leadership within the university.

Election objectives
